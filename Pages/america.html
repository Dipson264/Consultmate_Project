ACADEMIC PATHWAYS

Programs Available

From undergraduate to doctoral programs, find your perfect academic path

Undergraduate

Bachelor's degrees (4 years). Liberal arts education with major flexibility. Start after +2 completion.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Entry: After high school/+2
  • Test: SAT/ACT + TOEFL/IELTS

Master's Degree

Advanced specialization in your field. 1-2 year programs with research or coursework focus.

  • Duration: 1-2 years
  • Entry: After bachelor's
  • Test: GRE/GMAT + TOEFL/IELTS

PhD Programs

Research-intensive doctoral programs. Often fully funded with stipends and tuition waivers.

  • Duration: 4-6 years
  • Entry: After master's/bachelor's
  • Often fully funded

Pathway Programs

Community colleges and pathway programs. Affordable entry with transfer options to top universities.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Cost-effective pathway
  • Transfer to 4-year universities

Required Documents

Everything you need to prepare for a complete application

Academic Transcripts

Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions. Must be sealed and stamped by your school.

  • High school marksheets (+2)
  • Bachelor's degree transcripts
  • WES evaluation (if required)

Test Scores

Standardized test scores sent directly from testing agencies to universities.

  • TOEFL (80+) or IELTS (6.5+)
  • SAT/ACT (for undergrad)
  • GRE/GMAT (for graduate)

Essays & SOP

Personal statements and essays that showcase your story, goals, and fit with the program.

  • Statement of Purpose (500-1000 words)
  • Personal statement
  • Supplemental essays

Recommendations

2-3 letters from professors, employers, or mentors who can speak to your abilities.

  • Academic recommenders preferred
  • Professional letters accepted
  • Submitted through portals

Resume/CV

Comprehensive overview of your academic achievements, work experience, and extracurriculars.

  • 1-2 pages maximum
  • Include relevant experience
  • Highlight achievements

Passport & ID

Valid passport and identification documents for visa processing and enrollment.

  • Passport (6+ months validity)
  • Birth certificate
  • Citizenship certificate

Pro Tip: Start gathering documents at least 6 months before application deadlines to avoid last-minute stress!
